Arudhra Creations Private Limited

Women-led · Temple-inspired

Every day, South Indian temples receive beautiful flowers offered with reverence and devotion. Sacred garlands adorn the deities, fragrant petals spread across altars, and lotus blooms grace the sanctum. Yet by the next morning these same blessed flowers — once fragrant and full of life — are discarded as waste.

Arudhra Creations Private Limited believes these flowers deserve a second, sacred life. We are a women-led enterprise based in Salem, Tamil Nadu, committed to transforming temple-blessed flowers into premium, carbon-free agarbathi sticks that bring the divine essence of South Indian temples into everyday homes and sacred spaces.

Women-Led Innovation

A skilled women’s team leads our flower processing, fragrance blending, and hand-rolling, creating dignified livelihoods across Salem.

Salem-Rooted, Temple-Focused

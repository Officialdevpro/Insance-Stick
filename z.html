The Arudhra Story

A four-chapter journey of transformation and purpose

The Sacred Problem

Every day, thousands of tons of flowers offered in temples are discarded as waste. Shri C Valliappa, Managing Trustee of three ancient temples in Salem, observed this contradiction between devotion and environmental responsibility, sparking a vision for sustainable transformation.

A Vision Born

Research & Discovery

Mentoring Dr M E Paramasivam from Sona College of Technology, they employed design thinking methodology to explore solutions. Market analysis revealed that dried temple flowers could serve as premium, eco-friendly raw material for agarbathi production—a perfect blend of tradition and innovation.

Innovation Through Research

The Catalyst

Shri C Valliappa shared this opportunity with Mrs G C Saranya, an MCA graduate and software professional. Rather than returning to corporate employment, he mentored her toward social entrepreneurship—transforming potential barriers into opportunities for women-led business and community impact.

Women Leadership

Arudhra is Born

In August 2025, Arudhra Creations Pvt Limited was established as a women-led rural startup, recognized as a faculty startup under NEP 2020 at Sona College of Technology. The name "Arudhra" embodies compassion and sacred devotion—values that drive everything we create.

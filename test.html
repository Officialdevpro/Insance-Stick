What Makes 'Shri' Special

Premium Quality, Sacred Origins

Temple-Flower Based

Made exclusively from flowers blessed and offered at South Indian temples. Each stick carries the spiritual energy of temple offerings into your home.

Carbon-Free & Non-Charcoal

Crafted without charcoal, reducing smoke intensity and particulate emissions for a cleaner, gentler burning experience.

Long-Lasting Aroma

Approximately 9 inches per stick, providing consistent, pleasant fragrance for approximately 45 minutes under normal burning conditions.

Gentle on Senses

Specifically formulated to avoid common incense-related complaints. No eye irritation, allergic cough, or respiratory discomfort.

Ideal Room Coverage

Two sticks are generally sufficient to fragrance a room of approximately 500 sq. ft. Provides even, consistent fragrance distribution.

Eco-Friendly & Sustainable

Made from waste flowers that would otherwise be discarded. Biodegradable composition with zero landfill impact.

Women-Led Innovation

Arudhra Creations is managed by a dedicated women's team with expertise in flower processing, fragrance blending, and hand-rolling techniques. We provide dignified, meaningful livelihoods to women artisans across Salem and surrounding regions.

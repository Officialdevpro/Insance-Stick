The Sacred Journey

From temple blessing to fragrant stick — our four-step transformation process

1

Offered at South Indian Temples

Devotees and priests offer fresh, fragrant flowers at temples during daily archana, festivals, and special occasions.

2

Respectfully Collected

Before flowers are discarded, our team collects them directly from temple partnerships with reverence and care.

3

Gently Dried & Hand-Processed

Flowers are carefully dried under controlled conditions to preserve their natural fragrance compounds.

4

Rolled into 'Shri' Agarbathi

Each stick is hand-rolled by skilled artisans to approximately 9 inches in length, providing 45 minutes of continuous fragrance.

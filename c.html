Arudhra Creations Private Limited

Women-led · Temple-inspired

Every day, temples across South India receive beautiful flowers offered with devotion. Instead of becoming waste, Arudhra Creations gives these blessed blooms a respectful second life — transforming them into premium, carbon-free agarbathi sticks that bring the temple’s fragrance into homes and sacred spaces.
